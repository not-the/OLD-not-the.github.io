This Website

I made this website to use as a personal portfolio and to test my web development skills. A more complete list of web projects I've worked on is available on my Github profile. The privacy policy below is necessary for me to run ads on Carrot Clicker, however, I make an effort to preserve your privacy as much as possible on pages I don't run ads on.

Privacy Policy & Cookies

Last updated November 11, 2023

notkal.com uses cookies (localStorage) to provide basic client-side functionality such as themes and accessibility options. None of this data is sent or retained server-side. Google Search Console provides us with some bare-bones statistics about impressions and click-through rates from Google search. For all notkal.com subdomains without their own unique privacy policy, the same privacy & cookie policy also applies there. A description of third-party cookie usage & privacy is available below.

Third-party Cookies

Third parties, such as Google, use browser cookies to serve interest-based ads on notkal.com and its subdomains. Google's use of advertising cookies enables them and their partners to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites on the internet.

Opt out:

  • You may opt of of personalized advertising from Google by visiting their ad settings page: https://www.google.com/settings/ads

  • You can also opt out of personalized ads from other vendors here: http://www.aboutads.info/choices. You may need to disable your ad blocker or use the tool multiple times for every listed vendor to receive your request.

Changes to this Privacy Policy
This privacy policy is subject to change. A notice will appear on www.notkal.com with a link to this page with the updated policy.
